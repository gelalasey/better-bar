<li>1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil</li>
<li>2 teaspoons minced garlic</li>
<li>1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes</li>
<li>2 cups low sodium chicken or vegetable broth (or water)</li>
<li>1/2 teaspoon salt</li>
<li>1 tablespoon unsalted butter</li>
<li>1-1/2 cups (10 oz) couscous</li>
<li>Salt and freshly ground black pepper</li>
</ul></div>
</div>

![](/div/purplerose.gif)

### Instructions


1. Heat oil in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat. 
2. Saute garlic with red pepper flakes for 2 minutes being careful not to burn garlic. 
3. Combine the water or broth, salt, and butter. Stir in to pan and bring ingredients to a boil. 
4. Stir in the couscous, cover tightly with a lid, and remove from heat. 
4. Let the couscous steam for 5 minutes or until tender. All water should be absorbed by the end.
5. Use a fork to fluff the couscous and break up any clumps. Serve warm.
