## In a Nutshell 
#Lovesick is a 9 member jpop boy group with a "menhara" (mental illness core) theme that is very online, feminine, and transgressive in everyway possible. This company positions a lot of their boy groups as approachable to their feminine demographic by making their boys as soft and non-threatening as they can while still appealing to the het feminine gaze. The music sits in that nice inbetween of the nightcore and hyperpop eras. If you want the fraternity and charm of a boy group while still getting that girly aesthetic you'll want to check them out.
